The World's Best Cheese Ball

This cheese ball will make you want to get some crackers.

Ingredients

  • 1 amount of cheese
  • Some amount of seasonings
  • A dapper hat

Instructions

  1. Roll amount of cheese into a sphere.
  2. Sprinkle seasonings on floor.
  3. Roll sphere on seasoned floor until coated.
  4. Leave cheese sphere outside for stray cats.
